Describe gifs (poorly) with your friends!

Each round, one player (the Prompter) writes a short prompt to describe one of their gifs. Everyone else chooses a gif from their own hand that best matches the prompt, and then tries to guess which gif came from the prompter.

How To Play

  • Type /gifoff to start or continue a game in that channel (type at anytime if you need a hint)
  • Click Next Turn to start a new round with a random prompter
  • The prompter will get a Choose Gif button, or they can pass or nominate a different prompter. To choose, write a short prompt, and a gif that goes with it. Prompts should be neither too specific or too obscure, you want only some players to pick your gif out of the lineup. Some examples: "Help! The grocery store is out of quinoa!" / "logging on to twitter"
  • Once the prompter has chosen, everyone else has 10 minutes to choose a gif from their own hand that also matches the prompt. Their goal is to trick the other players into thinking that their gif came from the prompter
  • After 10 minutes, it's time to vote!
  • Anyone who chose a gif should click Vote to see all the chosen gifs, and vote for which one they think came from the prompter
  • Once the votes are in (or 10 minutes pass), all will be revealed, and the very important scores are tallied.
    • If everyone voted for the prompters gif (it was too obvious!) or no one does (it was too obscure!) then everyone except the prompter gets 2 points
    • Otherwise, the prompter & everyone who voted correctly gets 3 points
    • As a bonus, everyone else gets 1 point for each player they fooled with their gif
  • Repeat ad nauseam
